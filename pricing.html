FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan at any time?
Yes. You can change your plan at any time from your dealer dashboard. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades at the end of the current billing period.
Is there a long-term contract?
No. All plans are month-to-month (or annual if you choose). Cancel anytime with no penalty. We earn your loyalty by delivering value, not by locking you in.
How does the referral bonus work?
Each dealer gets a unique referral link. When a new dealer signs up and activates a paid plan using your link, you receive listing credits equivalent to one month of the plan they chose. Credits never expire.
What is the revenue share model?
Available on the Enterprise plan, revenue share means you pay a 5–10% commission on successfully facilitated sales, with no monthly subscription fee. This model is best suited for dealers with high-value, low-volume inventory. Contact us to discuss terms.
How does the free API inventory feed work?
On Elite and Enterprise plans, you can connect your existing inventory management system to LUL via our RESTful API or XML/CSV feed. Listings sync automatically so you never have to update manually. Full documentation and a dedicated onboarding call are provided.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), SEPA bank transfer, and PayPal. Annual invoices are also available for Enterprise clients.
Do you offer discounts for non-profits or startups?
Yes — we evaluate requests on a case-by-case basis. Please reach out via the contact form or email us directly at the address listed in the footer.
